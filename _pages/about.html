I am currently a Ph.D. student in computer vision and machine learning at the Australian National University under the primary supervision of Professor Richard Hartley. I am also working closely with Dr. Thalaiyasingam Ajanthan during my Ph.D. study. My research interests include Markov random field optimization with applications of semantic segmentation and stereo vision. I also spent some time on 3D neural network pruning. Before that, I was focused on some industrial works in China, and studied in signal and image processing under the primary supervision of Associate Professor Lei Zhang at Xidian University, now at Sun Yat-sen University. I am currently living in the beautiful bush city, Canberra, the capital of Australia.
