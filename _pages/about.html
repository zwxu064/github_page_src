Zhiwei is a Ph.D. candidate in computer vision and machine learning at the Australian National University under the supervision of Professor Richard Hartley and Dr. Thalaiyasingam Ajanthan. His research interests include Markov random field optimization, semantic segmentation, stereo vision, and 3D neural network pruning. Before that, he was focused on some industrial works in China, and studied in signal and image processing under the supervision of Associate Professor Lei Zhang, now also a professor at Sun Yat-Sen University, and Professor Mengdao Xing at Xidian University. He is currently living in the beautiful bush city, Canberra, the capital of Australia.
